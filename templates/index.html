Photo Guidelines for Better Results

Follow these tips to ensure our AI can provide the most accurate analysis.

Key Photo Guidelines

  • Image Ratio (3:4): Upload a head-to-knee photo with a 3:4 aspect ratio for best detection accuracy.
  • Neutral Pose: Stand straight, facing the camera directly, with your arms slightly away from your body.
  • Fitted Clothing: Wear relatively fitted clothing so your natural contours are visible to the AI.
  • Good Lighting: Ensure you are in a brightly and evenly lit space.
  • Clear Background: Use a plain, uncluttered background.
  • Image Quality: Use high-resolution, clear, and unblurry images for the best results.
